Spoons (TD Mixed With Restaurant Management)

Spoons (Play on Bloons) is a tower defense game inspired by both Bloons TD and Overcooked. In Spoons you manage a restaurant where you create custom waves of ingredients to defend against. When you defeat an enemy, instead of many, you collect them as ingredients. You can then use these ingredients cook recipes which in turn reward you with money to buy more towers. The goal of the game is to cook all of necessary food before you run out of waves. This challenges the player by forcing them to find a balance between too many enemies and enough ingredients to cook all of the necessary food. This game was written in C# and uses Unity. Everything in this game was designed by me, besides the sound effects.

Deep Divide

Three Guys and a Boat

Deep Divide is a two-player co-op survival game where you and a friend run a diving operation on the open ocean. One of you dives into the water to scavenge the sea floor for treasure chests while dodging sharks, and the other stays on the boat to watch oxygen levels and pull up the diver — oh and also manning the cannon when pirates show up. The catch is the diver can't see their own oxygen, so you have to actually talk to each other to survive. At the end of each day you sell your loot to a merchant, buy upgrades, and pay your lease — because if you can't afford it, it's game over. It's chaotic, it's cooperative, and there's nothing quite like screaming at your friend to pull you up while a shark is closing in. This game was written in C# and uses Unity.
